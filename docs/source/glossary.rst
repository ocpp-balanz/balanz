Glossary
--------

.. glossary::
    :sorted:

    balanz
        An OCCP-based smart charging solution.

    OCPP
        Open Charging Communication Protocol. See more at `Open Charge Alliance <https://openchargealliance.org/protocols/open-charge-point-protocol>`_.

    CSMS
    CS
        Charge Service Management System/Central System, a system that manages the charging process for electric vehicles.

    EV
        Electric Vehicle, a vehicle powered by electricity.

    Charger
    Charge Point
        The Charge Point or Charger is the physical system where an electric vehicle can be charged.

    LC
        OCCP-defined Local Controller. Sits between the Charge Points and Central System and performs Smart Charging.

    OCPP-J 
        OCPP via JSON over WebSocket
